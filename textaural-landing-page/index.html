Tired of skimming? Listen instead.

Do you find it hard to stay focused when reading long articles or documents online? With our app, you don't have to force your eyes to follow lines of text anymore. Just highlight what you want to hear, click the button, and listen. Our app turns any webpage into clear, natural audio — so you can absorb content while walking, eating, or resting your eyes. You can even speed it up, slow it down, or pause anytime. No switching apps. No distractions. Just you, your thoughts, and the words — delivered by voice.

About Me

The creator behind Textaural

Marek Szkudelski

Founder & Developer

Hello, I'm Marek, a Senior Frontend Software Engineer at Allegro with over 8 years of professional experience in programming. I've been passionate about software development since I was 15, and throughout my career I've focused on building practical, user-focused tools.

I created Textaural out of a personal need: I often struggle to find time and focus to read online content, and I noticed that most existing text-to-speech solutions are either too expensive or unnecessarily complex. Textaural is my response — a lightweight, affordable, and effective way to listen to web content directly in your browser.

I believe software should solve real problems and be effortless to use. With Textaural, my goal is to deliver a seamless experience with minimal setup and maximum utility.
